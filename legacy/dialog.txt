======================================
===     Welcome to Proton MO2 Installer!
======================================

Instructions:
    1. Install your chosen game through Steam.
    2. Read these instructions carefully, and follow the prompts until finished.
    3. When the installer is complete, launch the game as normal.

Notes:
    - The installer may interact with Steam in various ways, this may include closing running Steam instances, and launching the game to inititalize the prefix. Do NOT interfere with this process or the installation will fail to complete.
    - Configuring the prefix can take a long time in some cases. Please be patient, the process will finish eventually.

Visit https://github.com/ralgar/proton-mo2-installer for more info.
